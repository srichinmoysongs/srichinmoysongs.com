Garden of Love-Light, The (Part 1)

A book of 50 songs, published August 1, 1974 Show comments

This was Sri Chinmoy’s first published songbook. On January 13, 1972, at a special function in New York, sixteen singers from the New York and Connecticut Sri Chinmoy Centres performed the fifty Bengali songs in this book.
