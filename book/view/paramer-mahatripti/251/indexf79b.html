Paramer Mahatripti

A book of 201 songs, published January 1, 2005 Show comments

The 201 songs in this book were written for Chidananda’s singing group between January 2002 and February 2004. They are what Sri Chinmoy calls ‘vocabulary songs’ and are very helpful for anyone interested in learning Bengali. Most of them were written during Sri Chinmoy’s annual Christmas/New Year trips abroad, but there are also many that were written in New York.
